About Me

My full name is Rifki Ilham Baihaki, born on July 20, 1996 in Jember, East Java. I have taken formal education at SD Al-Furqan Jember (2003-2009), SMPN 1 Jember (2009-2012), SMAN 3 Jember (2012-2015).

In 2015 I continued my undergraduate studies at the Department of Mathematics FMIPA, University of Jember. My field of interest during my undergraduate degree was computer science and optimization. Then in 2019 even semester I continued my Masters level study at the Department of Mathematics FSAD, Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology.

My field of interest at post graduate is deep learning and computer vision. Now I work as an Assistant Lecturer at University of Jember and Lecturer at Politeknik Negeri Jember.
